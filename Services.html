Blockchain

A blockchain is a series of sequential chunks of information based on a transparent ledger-system, a shared file that keeps track of all historical information. A blockchain ledger records data that’s been verified by a network of private computers Blockchain technology is still comparatively new, but people, start-ups and established businesses have all been experimenting with it.
